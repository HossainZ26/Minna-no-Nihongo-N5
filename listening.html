💡 Listening Tips

  • 🔁
    Repeat shadowing

    Listen once, then repeat what you hear. This improves pronunciation and memory.

  • 📝
    Take notes

    Write down words you recognize. Even one new word per audio is progress!

  • 🎯
    Focus on keywords

    Don't try to understand everything at once. Listen for numbers, names, and greetings first.

  • 📅
    Daily listening

    Even 10 minutes of Japanese audio each day builds your ear over time.
