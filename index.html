💡 Beginner Study Tips

📅 Daily Practice

Study for 15–30 minutes every day rather than cramming. Consistency beats intensity when learning a new language.

✍️ Write by Hand

Writing kana by hand builds muscle memory. Practice stroke order for あいうえお before moving forward.

🎵 Listen Daily

Expose yourself to Japanese audio every day. Even background listening trains your ear to the rhythm and sounds.
